Prestige is a turn-based combat game where players are required to use strategy to out maneuver their opponents to victory.
There are two modes a player can choose to play in: Story Mode or The Arena. In story mode you will play through a sequence of differing opponents, picking up new heroes and abilities along the way. Alternatively, players can choose to play in the arena, where they will be faced with wave after wave of enemies until their team is destroyed.
