Story Mode
Stage 1 - Choose Your First Hero
When you begin a new story, you will be given three starting heroes to choose from. Each of these heroes will fight differently and have their own strengths and weaknesses.
Once you choose a hero to start with, you will be able to customize their stats to best suit your play-style.
Stage 2 - Begin Your Story
Now that your first hero is ready, it's time to journey into the world! You will initially be met with small challenges that allow you to strengthen your hero.
The more you use a hero, the faster they will level up. When a character levels up they get more stat-points to assign, as well as occasionally learning new abilities.
Stage 3 - Pickup Friends Along The Way
Depending on how you treat people along your journey, you'll they opportunity to gain a large number of friends and add them to your team to fight for you.
Some battlefields allow both teams to fight with multiples characters at once, so it is advantageous to keep a large team.
Stage 4 - Ending Your Journey
Eventually your journey will come to an end, but this doesn't mean that you will never play with your heroes again!
You will be able to travel back to areas to use your new heroes in old areas where you didn't originally have. On top of that, there will constantly be new content added to explore.
